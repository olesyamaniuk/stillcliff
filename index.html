About the Game

In Dark City: Paris, you'll find yourself in a version of the city where everything feels a bit off, like something's not right. Strange events begin to unfold across the city, and it’s up to the player to investigate what’s really happening. As you explore and solve puzzles, you'll start piecing together what's really going on - and trust me, it's not what you'd expect.

Game Experience

It's all about the story

Each location feels like part of a larger narrative. Players don’t just search for objects — they piece together a mystery. We didn't want to rush you through it - there's so much creepy detail in these Paris scenes that you'll want to take your time and really look around.
